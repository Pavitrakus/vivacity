Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

1. Introduction

At Vivacity, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our video generation API and services.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect information about you in a variety of ways, including data you provide directly to us (such as account details and payment information), data collected automatically (such as IP addresses and browser characteristics), and data generated through your use of the API (such as prompts and rendered videos).

3. Use of Your Information

Having accurate information about you permits us to provide you with a smooth, efficient, and customized experience. Specifically, we may use information collected about you to generate your requested animations, process payments, and improve our rendering infrastructure.

4. Data Security

We use administrative, technical, and physical security measures to help protect your personal information. All rendered videos and prompt data are encrypted at rest and in transit.
